ROLE OF BARBER SHOP AND SALONS IN DAY TO DAY LIFE

ROLE OF BARBER SHOP AND SALONS IN DAY TO DAY LIFE

People feel good when they step out of the salon. A fresh haircut, some trendy highlights or even just a simple trim and shave can boost one’s confidence and add a little pep to one’s step. Clients leave the hair salon or barbershop excited over their new look, without knowing that the person behind the chair and any infected client can be a medium to transfer multiple diseases to them thus putting their health and safety at risk.

RISK AT BARBERS SHOP IN THE CURRENT SCENARIO
OF COVID-19 PANDEMIC

RISK AT BARBERS SHOP IN THE CURRENT SCENARIO OF COVID-19 PANDEMIC

  1. Barber’s shop and salons is the place where a number of people visit everyday.
  2. Though the barbers are advised to check the temperature of the employees and clients and to sterilize their tolls, the prescribed procedure is not followed everywhere and in the right manner.
  3. If an infected person visits the barber shop and get the procedure done ( shaving, hair cutting ,hair colouring etc)the virus can be transmitted to other people who visit the same barber shop and they can spread the disease further.
  4. For sterilization of tools only high end salons have infrared machines to sterilize their tools.
  5. The average sterilization time of barber tools is 25-30 minute, but due to the rush of clients, most of the times the barber compromise on the sterilization time thus compromising the health of the client.
  6. COMBS, SCISSORS, RAZORS, ALUM, BLADES, APPRON, TOWEL and most and almost all of the tools at a barbers shop is used on multiple people therefore it increase the possibility of transferring of disease from one person to many people.
  7. Currency notes transaction can also become a medium to transfer covid 19.
  8. Gathering of a number of people at the barbers shop to wait for their turn can also prove to be disastrous in the current scenario.
  9. Barbers not wearing new and unused hand gloves on every client have more possibility of transferring the disease.
  10. Since Apron cannot be sterilized in between procedures therefore the barber shop is very prone to transfer the disease from this medium.

MUST FOR SALON VISITS

MUST FOR SALON VISITS

It is highly recommended that the person should not visit the salon without safeguarding himself. One should understand that the tools at the barber ‘shop came into contact with the skin and hairs of a multiple people, which can became a medium of transferring not only corona virus but a number of fungus and bacterial diseases too . A little ignorance from the barber, can cost multiple people very dearly.

So why to expose yourself on the mercy of the barber and why not to carry your own equipments and tools to avoid even the slightest possibility of infection from contaminated tools and equipments.

MUST TO CHECK AND DO AT THE TIME OF VISITING A SALON

MUST TO CHECK AND DO AT THE TIME OF VISITING A SALON

The first step in reducing or eliminating the risks in a hair salon or barbershop is to check if all the employees are trained and their working area is organized , clean and safe or not. Are they mopping and cleaning the hair clippings immediately after a procedure or not.

It’s recommended that client use personal protective equipment barber (PPE) kit to keep common tools off the skin and to minimize the spread of virus and bacteria.

Temperature checks -please ensure that salon employees must be checking the temperature of their staff and the clients coming for procedure.

Client entering a salon/barber shop should check the following things.

  1. Does anyone in the shop have a cough?
  2. Does anyone in the shop have a fever?
  3. Avoid spending time in the shop/salon – Clients should consider seeing the barber by appointment only. Salons/shops should consider telephonic or online scheduling.
  4. It is recommended that clients wait outside the salon/shop in their vehicle until the cosmetologist or barber is ready to serve them.
  5. It is recommended that persons not being serviced in the salon wait outside the salon.
  6. Clients should maintain social distancing at all times! Spacing between persons in the salon should be at least six feet, except when staff are servicing clients.
  7. Try to make all the cashless transactions all the time.
  8. Ensure that the barber must sanitize the client's chair after every procedure with the help of sanitizer fogging machine or by other means.
