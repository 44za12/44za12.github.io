RANA CONCEPTS AND DESIGNS has been created to help and safeguard the people going to barber shops and salons in the current scenario of ongoing COVID-19 Pandemic.

WHO WE ARE:

The RCDS team has been formed to lay a helping hand to prevent COVID-19 and other communicating diseases which might spread from barber shops and salons unintentionally.

Our team has worked on every single aspect which can spread the disease from the barbers shop.

WHAT WE ARE TRYING TO ACHIEVE:

We know that customers can struggle to find suitable qualified and skilled barber. And sometimes even the most skilled barber and the most hygienic salons can miss one or two steps required to curb the spread of disease out of the act of negligence which can often lead to disastrous results. We as a team hopes to merge its efforts and skill for the specific purpose to curb and eliminate the spread of infectious diseases arising out of negligence from barbers shop and salons.

We believe in the proverb "Prevention is better than cure" and try to guide the people going to barbers shop ,measures to prevent themselves and their families from the spread of these deadly diseases.

Together , by using the Man of Valor kit we can become a fighter against the spread of COVID-19 and likewise diseases.

HOW WE WILL ACHIEVE THE GOAL:

All of the tools at the barbers shop and salons is made to be used on multiple people without taking into consideration their skin and hair conditions and the disinfection of these tools most of the times compromised which makes a number of people vulnerable to a number of diseases which may be transferred from an infected person. RCDS has understood this problem in detail and made a Personal Protection Equipment ( PPE ) Kit for the people going to the barbers shop for different procedures.

This kit consists of all the tools required by the barber for different procedures on face and hairs. The person can carry their personal kit in a state of the art bag to the barbers shop ,get the procedure done with the help of personal tools and carry it back therefore eliminating most of the possibilities of any disease transferred from improper use of contaminated tools.

The kit carries the following items:

  • PAPER TOWELS
  • HIGH QUALITY RAZOR (Professional)
  • BEST OF THE BLADES
  • HANDGLOVES
  • ANTISEPTIC ALUM
  • CARRY BAG
  • APRON
  • SCISSORS
  • COMB
  • HAIR CLIPS
  • HAIR BAND
  • HAIR DYEING BRUSH
